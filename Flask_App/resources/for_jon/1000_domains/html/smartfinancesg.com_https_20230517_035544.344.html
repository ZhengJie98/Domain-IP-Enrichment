FAQ

You can learn more from our asked questions

At Smart Finance, it only takes 30 minutes to approve your personal loans.

What is Smart Finance?

Smart Finance is a licensed moneylender provider in Singapore that offers customized loan solutions to individuals and businesses in need of fast and reliable financial assistance.

What types of loans do you offer?

We offer a variety of loan solutions, including personal loans, business loans, fast cash loans, debt consolidation loans, bad debt loans, and renovation loans.

What are the eligibility criteria for a loan?

To be eligible for a loan, you must be a Singaporean citizen, permanent resident, or foreigner with a valid work permit. You must also be over the age of 21 and meet our credit assessment requirements.

What are your interest rates?

Our interest rates are competitive and in compliance with the regulations set forth by the Ministry of Law. The exact rate will depend on the loan amount and repayment terms.

What happens if I can't make my loan repayments on time?

If you are unable to make your loan repayments on time, please contact us as soon as possible. We will work with you to find a suitable solution to help you meet your financial obligations.

How much can I borrow?

The loan amount you can borrow will depend on your income and creditworthiness. We offer loans ranging from S$500 to S$100,000.

How do I apply for a loan with Smart Finance?

You can apply for a loan with Smart Finance by filling out our online application form or visiting our office during business hours. Our loan specialists will guide you through the application process and provide you with all the necessary information to make an informed decision.

How long does it take to get approved for a loan?

Our loan application process is fast and easy. We typically approve loans within 24 hours of receiving all the necessary documentation.

How do I make loan repayments?

You can make loan repayments through various methods, including bank transfer, GIRO, or in-person at our office.

Is my personal information safe and secure?

Yes, we take your privacy and security seriously. We use the latest technology and protocols to protect your personal information and only share it with authorized parties as required by law.

If you have any additional questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact us. Our friendly and knowledgeable team is always ready to assist you.
