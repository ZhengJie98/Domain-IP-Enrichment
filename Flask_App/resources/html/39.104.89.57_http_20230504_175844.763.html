Research Direction

Welcome to the Dong’s Group of Inner Mongolia Agricultural University. Our research interests mainly focus on:

Identification of Antimicrobial peptides: Since 2019, we have focused on the prediction of antimicrobial peptides, especially the prediction of antimicrobial peptides of lactic acid bacteria, the construction of database and the development of recognition platform(Dong et al, Frontiers in Genetics, 2021).

Research on Algorithm Design and Parallelization of Gene Sequence Splicing and Alignment: We design and implement parallel gene sequence splicing algorithm in spark, and also implement parallel alignment algorithm based on Center Star in spark. These two algorithms have a significant improvement in the execution time on the basis of ensuring the stitching accuracy and alignment score(Dong, International Journal of Pattern Recognition and Artificial Intelligence, 2017,31(8);Dong et al, Journal of Computational Methods in Sciences and Engineering,2018,18(1):283-295;Dong et al, Journal of Computational Methods in Sciences and Engineering,2016,16(3):519-526).

algorithm and Its Parallelization for combinatorial optimization problems: This direction focuses on the design and parallelization of intelligent algorithms for combinatorial optimization problems. A hybrid ant colony genetic algorithm was designed to solve the classical combinatorial optimization problem (TSP, Traveling Salesman Problem), and the optimal solutions were achieved in many instances in the international database TSPLIB(Dong et al, Expert Systems with Applications,2012,39(5):5006-5011; Dong et al, Computer & Electrical Engineering,2017,60：66-75;Dong et al, International Journal of Advancements in Computing Technology,2012,4(5):165-171).
