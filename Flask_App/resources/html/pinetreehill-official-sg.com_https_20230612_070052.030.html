The Best of Both Worlds at Pinetree Hill

Nestled in the heart of the highly sought-after Pine Grove neighbourhood, Pinetree Hill offers the best of both worlds.

Residents will enjoy the tranquillity and serenity of being surrounded by lush greenery, while also being just a stone’s throw away from a wide range of top-notch amenities. With five major commercial hubs in close proximity, Pinetree Hill is an ideal location for those who value convenience and accessibility.

At Pinetree Hill, every detail has been carefully thought out to ensure the utmost comfort and convenience for our residents. Our site plan boasts a range of top-notch facilities, including a 50m lap pool, children’s pool, fitness corner, and clubhouse. Residents can also enjoy stunning views of the surrounding neighbourhood from the sky terrace on the 14th floor. In addition, Pinetree Hill’s site plan includes ample green spaces, with lush landscaping throughout the development, providing a serene and peaceful environment for residents to unwind and relax.

We have also incorporated a number of practical amenities, such as a car park, bicycle park, and drop-off porch, ensuring that residents’ daily needs are well taken care of. With its well-thought-out site plan, Pinetree Hill offers residents a luxurious and comfortable lifestyle that is truly second to none.
