Welcome to our website

Dr.Sachin Vaze,  one of the most reputed gastrointestinal surgeons in Pune, is well known and appreciated for his personalized, down to earth approach towards his patients.

Being a trained endoscopist as well, he offers a comprehensive service that includes prevention and diagnosis, in addition to surgical treatment, for complicated diseases of the pancreas, biliary tract, gastrointestinal tract, liver and spleen.

He leverages his special training in endoscopy and advanced laparoscopy to provide patients better surgical care in terms of outcomes, minimal discomfort and reduced hospital stay.

Dr. Vaze is committed to evidence based treatment whether it is a simple appendectomy or a complex procedure such as Ileal pouch anal anastomosis, G.I. cancers. He is also very particular about how his patients and their relatives are looked after by his team. Clarity in communication, a calm and unhurried approach and sensitivity to their concerns is a hallmark of DrVaze and his team's approach.

His genuine concern for his patients and their relatives is seen in the effort he takes to ensure his availablity for them when needed.

  • Dr. Vaze also offers preventive diagnostic endoscopy services to patients who are at high risk of developing gastro-intestinal due to chronic consumption of tobacco and / or alcohol, as also to individuals with a strong family history of gastro-intestinal cancers.

  • Comprehensive care for patients suffering from intestinal tuberculosis, chronic diarrhoea, Ulcerative Colitis, Crohn's Disease, colon cancer, bleeding from back passage, chronic constipation and ano-rectal problems.
