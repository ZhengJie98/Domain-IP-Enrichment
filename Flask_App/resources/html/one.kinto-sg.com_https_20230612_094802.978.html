FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What Are The Benefits of Leasing Compared to Just Buying a Car?

With the rising COE prices, weve seen a surge in demand for our cars because of the significant cost-efficiencies.

Many of our clients find that it makes more economical sense to lease because of the following factors:

  • No large downpayment - pay less upfront to free up funds for other investments and lifestyle needs
  • Road Tax
  • Unlimited Mileage
  • We cover servicing & mechanical maintenance
  • Free Delivery and Collection
  • 24-Hrs Roadside Assistance

Instead of forking out large sums for a depreciating asset, we provide a flexible approach thats cost-effective, stress-free and comes with no hidden fees!

Why should I lease from Kinto One instead of others?

Were all about having the best customer experience on the market.

When you sign with us you arent just getting a dependable car with powerful features, you also receive a full-service exclusive membership that entitles you to so much more!

  • No Claim Discount (NCD) privilege - all existing insurer discounts can be carried forward to your future car
  • Insurance excess
  • Complimentary concierge service
  • Exclusive fuel discount card
  • 24/7 roadside assistance
  • Unbeatable after-sales service

We take care of you & your car, whilst you kick-back and enjoy all the benefits without the hassle.

Can I try out the car before signing?

Sure, we'd love to show you around!

You can head down to our office at 22 Sin Ming Lane, Midview City, #01-77 Singapore 573969.

Is there anybody I can talk to that can guide me through the process?

To ensure you get the best response and personalised service, we will assign a dedicated accounts servicing specialist for you!

Well assess your current & future needs to make recommendations that will work for you long-term.

Im not sure which car suits my needs the best?

Fret not, all of our accounts servicing staff are well-trained to help you access your budget requirements, family needs, personal needs as well as future requirements.

Our flexible lease durations allow you to upgrade your car models to cater for your needs. Reach out to us and well get you started!
