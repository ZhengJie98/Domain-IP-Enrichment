LEAVE THE ORDINARY BEHIND AND COME TO PARADISE

A perfect place to call home, the luxurious new condo development of Lentor Hills Residences on Lentor Hills Road offers spacious housing and stunning views for residents. The Lentor Hills Residences development was sold to a joint venture between GuocoLand, Leong Holdings, and TID for S$585.6 million, or S$1,060 per square foot per plot ratio. With a total site area of about 17,100 square meters and a maximum Gross Floor Area (GFA) of 60,480 square meters, this development boasts an expected 598 residential units. Located in the desirable neighbourhood of Ang Mo Kio in District 20, this condo development is surrounded by established transportation options and amenities such as schools, shopping malls, and medical centres. Additionally, the neighbourhood is home to luxurious and roomy Housing and Development Board towns, making it suitable for large families. Discover this exciting launch of Lentor Hills Condo at Lentor Hills Road, where you can experience the best of comfort and convenience in an elegant setting.

Review on Lentor Hills Residences

Lentor Hills Residences is a new project that will be launched this year. The Lentor Hills Road Condominium (Parcel A) is now on the URA GLS site list and is scheduled to go to tender in September 2021. The site size is 17,100 m2, and the Gross Plot Ratio is 3.0. This 99-year leasehold project is projected to provide 598 residential units. The Lentor Hills Road Residences are located next to Lentor Drive, Flossia Park, and Yio Chu Kang Road in Singapore’s Northern Region, on the boundary of Thomson, Yishun, Ang Mo Kio, Sin Ming, Bishan, and the Central Water Catchment. The construction is close to the Thomson-East Coast Line’s future Lentor MRT station – Lentor Hills Residence

The Future of Ang Mo Kio and How It Affects Lentor Hills Residences Residents

The Future of Ang Mo Kio and How It Affects Lentor Hills Residences Residents Home to mature industrial estates, lively commercial centres, dynamic new towns and rustic islands, Singapore’s North-East region is a diverse area that offers a perfect balance of both urban and suburban lifestyle. With the 2019 URA Master Plan in place, exciting developments are set to take place in Ang Mo Kio.

Changes include completing the Cross Island Line (CRL) and the Thomson-East Coast Line (TEL) by 2035, expanding walking and cycling networks and increasing green spaces. Other developments consist of new housing units in Kebun Baru, Lentor Hills Road and Ang Mo Kio Ave 1, as well as more job opportunities in surrounding commercial centres and industrial estate. The cumulative effect of these transformations will make Ang Mo Kio an even more attractive residential area for investors and HDB upgraders looking for private properties, thereby pushing the value of Lentor Hill Residences.
