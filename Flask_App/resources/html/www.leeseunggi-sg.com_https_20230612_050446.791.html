2023 Lee Seung Gi Asia Tour Concert in Singapore - Direction to Resort World Sentosa Convention Center

Hi Airen,

10 years ago, we directed Airen to see meet Seung Gi at the Festive Grand Theatre in Resorts World™ Sentosa for 2013 Lee Seung Gi “The Brilliant Show “ In Singapore. 10 years later, we are back at Resorts World™ Sentosa but a bigger venue, Convention Centre. Let's begin!

Please note that Sentosa is an island, a short walk away from Singapore's mainland.

How to get to Resorts World™ Sentosa from Singapore Mainland.
Please refer to this link below for a comprehensive guide to getting to Resort World, Sentosa via the Public Bus Services, MRT, Taxi, Cars and even via a good walk.
They also provided links to the MRT maps.

HOW TO GET TO RESORT WORLD SENTOSA, CLICK HERE- > http://www.rwsentosa.com/language/en-US/Homepage/HowToGetHere

A commonly used mode of transport to Sentosa is via the Sentosa Express (a light rail system).
Please refer to this for more information: https://www.sentosa.com.sg/en/getting-to-sentosa/

Sentosa Express is located within a shopping mall, Vivocity. Below is a photo guide on how to hop on the Sentosa Express to RWS.

Download the Rail Map / Journey Planner app to get around in Singapore via MRT. 








Enter your start location (e.g. Changi Airport) - and destination (Harbourfront MRT), they provide the route on how to get there.

The commonly used app for taxi and a private hire car is Grab and CDG Zig App. 

a) Exit Harbourfront MRT Station and you will be at Vivocity Shopping Mall. You will see this store “Candy Empire” when you exit. Please take the escalator right outside this store and proceed up to level 3 (Top most level).



b) At level 3, you will see the Sentosa Express entrance



c) If you have an MRT Ezlink card or Credit Card/Master with Ezlink logo behind the card, you can use it to take the ride. It will deduct $4 (round-trip) from the card. If you don’t have any of the card, please purchase a ticket using the steps below.



d) Head over to the ticket counters or the self-purchase machine to purchase sentosa express ticket.



Step 1: Purchasing your ticket. 1 ticket - $4 (Singapore Dollar).

 Step 2: Click [$4.00 Island Asmission]
Step 3: Click the number of ticket you wish to purchase. 1 person = 1 ticket
Step 4: Get ready to put in your money when you see this screen.
Step 5: Insert your money
Step 6: Collect your ticket =)

e) Taking the Sentosa Express. Enter the gate by tapping your ticket.



g) Please alight at the Waterfront Station. It is just 1 stop away.



Upon reaching Waterfront Station, you are now at Resorts World™ Sentosa.

Please follow the steps below to get to the Convention Centre.
Exit Waterfront Station and you will see signage that points you towards the Casino, Festive Grand and Convention Centre. Please follow the arrow.



Turn left and you will be pointed to an escalator that brings you down to ground level where the Casino is. Please take the escalator down.


You will see Festive Grand Theatre on your left which they have covered up the signage and is currently showing Van Gogh Exhibition.


Walk further down, you will see Toast Box, keep walking. 



















And you will see the Convention Centre!























If you need a locker for luggage, you can find a luggage locker in the nearby washroom beside ToastBox.


































Let's have a great time with Seung Gi in Singapore!
