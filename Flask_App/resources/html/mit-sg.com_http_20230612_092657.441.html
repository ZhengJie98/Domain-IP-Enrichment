ABOUT US
Measuring Instrument Technology Pte Ltd (MIT) was established in 1995 with the objective of serving the increasingly sophisticated needs of industries in the Asia-Pacific region for accurate, dependable and durable measuring instruments.
Today, MIT is a major sole agency and distributor for a comprehensive range of mechanical and metrological testing instruments for end-use product manufacturers and their supporting contract manufacturers of springs, connectors, cellular telephones, plastics and rubber products, electronics and electrical products as well as various automotive components.
Managed and staffed by a group of dedicated and personable professionals, MIT stocks a comprehensive range of state-of-the-art instruments from Japan, Germany and the United States, specialising in the non-destructive as well as destructive measurement and testing of force, dimension and hardness.
Headquartered in Singapore, MIT covers South East Asia region as a sole distributor of various distinctive brands. With affiliated subsidiaries in China and Vietnam, it can focus on the provision of a more pro-active marketing and after-sales services to its clients. These after-sales services include application support, calibration and re-calibration, and repair which can be conducted either on site or in-house.
MIT’s valued clients include reputable multi-national corporations group such as Panasonic (Matsushita), Canon, Brother, Molex, Epson, Advanex (Kato Spring), Exedy, FCC, NHK whose continued custom and close working relationship throughout the years have been testimonial to MIT’s uncompromising standards and expertise in this specialised field.
