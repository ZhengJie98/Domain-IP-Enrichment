Located at the foothills of the Bukit Timah Nature Reserve, The Reserve Residences is thoughtfully curated and features a dynamic blend of 1- to 5-bedroom luxury residences, serviced residences, shops, F&B, community and public spaces, with direct access to Beauty World MRT Station and the upcoming bus interchange; a well-integrated urban community node in the heart of Bukit Timah that will contribute to the vibrancy of the existing precinct.

— Your Community —

Strategic location in the lively Beauty World neighbourhood: Perfect location for young professionals and families with easy access to amenities. Considered as a multi-use development with an integrated transport hub to "enhance Beauty World's identity as an urban green village with excellent public transport connectivity."

Well-connected neighbourhood and just a short distance from the vibrant CBD. Within a short walk away is Beauty World MRT which runs on the Downtown Line, it brings residents to the CBD within a few minutes. It is also connected to Bukit Timah Road, Dunearn Road, and major expressways like BKE, PIE, and KJE.

A new peek of luxurious facilities

— Site plan & Facilities —

Your home at The Reserve Residences provides a privileged opportunity to own a space in a sought-after neighbourhood. Offering a diverse mix of restaurants, boutiques, bars, and cafés all within reach, residents across the development’s 700 units will enjoy accessibility and exclusivity few can call their own. Upon arrival, nature invites you in. Stroll through the terrace gardens, or be greeted by the tranquil waterfall courtyard lined with a canopy of greens hanging above the arrival court.

CREATE FOND MEMORIES IN ONE SPLENDOROUS LANDSCAPE

Whether it is time spent with loved ones frolicking in the pool, or a quiet respite at the Hammock Lawn and Relaxation Alcove, One-North Eden provides ample space for you to live your life the way you desire.

INDULGE IN INFINITE LEISURE AND REPOSE OF NATURE

Inspired by the native Zingiber singapurense, commonly known as the Singapore Ginger, specially customised Ginger Bud Gazebos can be found along the 50m lap pool deck, adding an unique charm to the landscape.
