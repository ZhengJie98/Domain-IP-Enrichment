About the brand.

No Starving Artists.

NSA is dedicated to helping dancers of all ages reach their next level. Were doing this through in-person & online training as well as education via the No Starving Artists Podcast.

Our plan is to continue growing & providing opportunities for dancers to do what they love without being a starving artist.

About me.

Jason Pickett has spent the last 11 years teaching competitive dance & dancing professionally.

He has performed on film and in live events around the world for companies like: Herms, Icon Fitness, Toys R Us, Crocs and Bud Light, Americas Got Talent & more!

You can also catch Jason in several viral YouTube videos including Stormtrooper Twerk and High School Dance Battle Geeks vs. Cool Kids! (4k).

Additionally, he is a former cast member with Odyssey Dance Theatre under the direction of Derryl Yeager and participated in their international tour to Germany and Israel.

His choreography has been featured in several viral YouTube videos as well as in several nationally ranked competitive routines. Currently, Jason is working on growing his online training platform for dancers, No Starving Artists, which is dedicated to taking dancers to their next level through movement exploration, stage presence work & finding the HOW within their dancing.
