FAQ



How to be eligible for the loan?

Here are the basic requirements for applying a loan with us:

  • Singapore Citizen or PR
  • Foreigners: Passport & Valid Work Permit
  • 21 years old or older, and is not an undischarged bankrupt, nor facing any bankruptcy proceeding
  • With Full-time Job or Self-employed with Notice of Assessment (NOA)

How to apply?

Fill up the form above. Or you email us. 


How much can I borrow?

We provide both unsecured and secured loans. 

For unsecured loans, you can get:

  • Annual income less than $20,000: Up to $3000
  • Annual income $20,000 or more but less than $30,000: Up to 2 months’ income
  • Annual income $30,000 or more but less than $120,000: Up to 4 months’ income
  • Annual income $120,000 or more: Any amount

For secured loans, you can get a loan of any amount

 


What documents do you require?

For Singapore citizen 21 years old and above, you require: 

  • NRIC
  • Proof of income and 
  • Proof of residence

For Foreigner, you require: 

  • Valid work/employment permit
  • Tenancy agreement and 
  • Proof of residenc
