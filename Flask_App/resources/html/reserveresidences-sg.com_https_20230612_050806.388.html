about the developer

Sino Group

Sino Group is one of the leading property developers in Hong Kong. In addition to an extensive portfolio in Hong Kong, the Group has footprints in Mainland China, Singapore and Australia. The Group has developed more than 220 projects, spanning a total plot ratio area of over 84.6million sq ft. With a team of over 10,000 dedicated professionals, Sino Group seeks to consistently deliver quality properties and services to surpass the expectations of customers, building a better community together.

Far East Organization

Far East Organization is a Christian Enterprise, which develops real estate and operates businesses by serving with grace, love, integrity and honesty. Far East Organization is the largest private property developer in Singapore with operations in Malaysia and Australia. It has developed over 780 developments across all segments of real estate in Singapore since its establishment in 1960. Far East Organization is the winner of 13 FIABCI World Prix d’Excellence awards, the highest honour in international real estate.
