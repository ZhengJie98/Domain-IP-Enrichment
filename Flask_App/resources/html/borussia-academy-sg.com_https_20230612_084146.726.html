At Borussia we believe that we not only train young footballers, but we are also coaching young people in their overall development and growth.

We accept and encourage children of all abilities, and Borussia coaches attend to the  different individual needs and talents of every player.

It is our priority to guarantee every participant a positive experience in our training sessions and tournaments. Fun, enjoyment & maximum participation are the main ingredients of all Borussia sessions.

Borussia coaching employs age-specific training programs that focus on skill development, motor coordination and the fundamentals of team play.

Borussia players do not stand in a queue to await their turn every few minutes. The small games approach (1v1,2v2,4v4) is particularly crucial and effective for the younger age groups (4 - 9), and ensures maximum engagement and contact with the ball for every player. Borussia training methods encourage players to be creative thinkers and decision makers in a variety of game situations. There are no boring, monotonous, repetitive drills !

Once young players are ready, we will provide them with match play experiences without pressure to win.

We focus on the playersâ long term development, not short term results, and our parents are most supportive

All Borussia coaches are certified and qualified, and follow a detailed, age- specific training plan.

Coaches are the key to a successful football program and Borussia players deserve a high quality, well planned and well executed training program.

In addition, Borussia coaches attend monthly training workshops and are required to undertake regular theoretical and practical assessments, which ensure our training sessions and coaching are of the highest quality.
