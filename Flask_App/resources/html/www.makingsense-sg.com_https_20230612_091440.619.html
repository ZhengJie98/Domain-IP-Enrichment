At Making Sense

It’s all about finding a perfect balance between the passion and joy of teaching and achieving academic excellence. We believe that every student has their unique learning needs that change with time.

Over the last 10 years, we have dedicated efforts to refining our curriculum and pedagogy, empowering students to learn effectively while producing the best academic results.

As a testimony to our superior education services for Secondary and JC students, we have firmly established ourselves as an award-winning Chemistry tuition centre, serving the needs of Secondary School Chemistry / O Level Chemistry and A Level JC Chemistry students in Singapore.

We have come a long way from our humble beginning with only 32 students in 2011. Today with our vast reserve of teaching experience and dedicated team, we are privileged to have the opportunity to coach close to 1000 students yearly.

We are committed to provide quality learning

At Making Sense, we provide only the best Science and Chemistry tuition in Singapore, from Secondary 1 and 2 Science to O-Level Chemistry and A-Level Chemistry students.

With our team of first-class Chemistry tutors and Science tutors, every lesson is designed to be concise, bite-size and productive.

We plan our time efficiently, giving our students a sense of accomplishment after every session. Whether you are a JC H2 / H1 Chemistry student / Secondary School Chemistry / O-Level Chemistry student preparing for national exams, or an enthusiastic IP Chemistry student learning foundational A Level Chemistry or even a lower Secondary Science student, we can help you with your needs. Our team has worked with thousands of similar students and  we will be able to help you excel.

We guarantee value for money

Price is what you pay for, value is what you receive in return. As the leading JC Chemistry, IP Chemistry, O Level Chemistry, and Science tuition centre, our obligations include providing as much value to our students and helping them excel in every way possible.

With an excellent team of Science and Chemistry tutors, well-developed resources, proven pedagogy, stellar track records and strong after-class support, we would like to extend our expertise to as many Secondary and JC Chemistry students as possible.

We believe that everyone is capable of mastering O Level and A Level Chemistry and Science and we want to help you succeed.

We stay relevant to the current syIIabus

We are probably the only Secondary School Chemistry /  JC Chemistry tuition centre with a full-fledged team of curriculum specialists, working closely with our team of Chemistry tutors to plan, develop and produce quality curriculum resources.

Topics ranging from Inorganic Chemistry, Physical Chemistry and Organic Chemistry have been extensively researched to allow us to craft out engaging and meaningful learning experiences for students in our Chemistry tuition / Science tuition programs.

We provide unique learning experiences

lt is our vision to make the learning of Chemistry and Science dynamic, inspiring and fun. Our curriculum and teaching team has spent countless hours designing pre-lesson interactive videos, flash cards, teaching aids and gamifying quizzes/assessments.

We want students to come to class excited and ready to explore the fascinating possibilities that Chemistry and Science can offer to our lives.

Why Making Sense?

You are Far More Likely to Score ‘A’

Year after year, we have produced exceptional results. Approximately 90% of our JC Chemistry students achieve A or B grade for their A-Level Chemistry exam and 92% of our Secondary school students achieved at least a B3 for their O-Level Chemistry exam.

Save Half Your Time With Optimised Learning

Our notes are comprehensive and concise. With a strong emphasis on application, we constantly expose students to common exam questions. You will also learn the best techniques to effectively solve challenging questions. Content heavy topics such as Organic Chemistry will be repackaged and simplified.

Work With Only The Best & Have A Peace of Mind

We are not a new one-man tuition centre. Over the past decade, we have firmly established ourselves as the leader in Science tuition and Chemistry tuition. Every year, over 800 students flock to Making Sense to be coached by us. We have successfully transformed the grades of over 8000 students.

Support From a Team of Experts

The Making Sense pedagogy is replicated among our tutors. All our Chemistry tutors and Science tutors speak, explain and deliver the same way, ensuring lessons of the highest quality.

Flexible Schedule

With multiple time slots, it will be easy to find a replacement class within the same week. Students can also have a choice of attending make ups at different locations across Singapore.

Strong After-Class Support

Reach out to our tutors whenever necessary. Students enjoy free Telegram consultations and face-to-face consultation sessions. Going the extra mile and providing more value is what we aim for.
